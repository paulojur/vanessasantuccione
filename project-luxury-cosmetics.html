Luxury Cosmetics

The Brief

A vibrant digital reinterpretation of Botticelli's Birth of Venus, designed to communicate a message of Inherent Worth, Feminine Power, and Self-Actualization. The work asserts that every woman embodies the archetype of the Goddess (Deusa), emphasizing confidence and innate value.

The Concept

The Rebirth of the Inner Goddess. This concept updates the classical ideal of beauty and love with themes of modern female empowerment. By isolating the figure of Venus against a vivid pink frame and surrounding her with intense flora and fauna (the Hoopoe bird as a symbol of self-respect), the piece visually demands recognition. It transforms the mythological narrative into a declaration that every woman's emergence (in her career, life, or self-identity) is a moment of divine, powerful (Ri)Nascita (Rebirth).
