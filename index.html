Vanessa Santuccione is a Brazilian digital artist based in Italy, known for her visionary collages that collaborate with fashion and luxury brands worldwide. With a background in Law and a multidisciplinary practice spanning painting, poetry, and analog collage, she has exhibited internationally and performed live art in Europe.

Her work embraces chaos as perfect order, transforming the unseen into poetic visual narratives.
