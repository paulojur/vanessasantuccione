Travel & Lifestyle

The Brief

A minimalist digital collage utilizing maritime allegory to depict the themes of Emotional Mastery and Self-Discovery. The image communicates a decisive shift from emotional overwhelm to using inner feelings as a functional guide for personal direction.

The Concept

A visual metaphor for Navigating the Self. The schooner (representing the individual) faces, yet calmly sails past, a powerful wave (raw emotion), signifying control and integration rather than avoidance. Key elements, such as the visible reflection, the subtle compass, and the soaring butterflies, establish a narrative of conscious direction, profound transformation, and the vast potential realized through inner exploration.

Personal Project

Identity Exploration

The Brief

A surrealist digital composition that explores the themes of Vulnerability as Strength and Authentic Self-Expression. The piece uses the anatomical heart and tropical flora to suggest that true personal and professional growth stems from nurturing one's internal, core self.

The Concept

The Blossoming Core. The central image of the anatomical heart is transformed into a vessel from which vibrant tropical plants (Bird of Paradise) emerge. This visually connects the act of being truly oneself ('free to be me') with organic, powerful growth. The heart, often hidden and vulnerable, is deliberately positioned on a stable, grounded base (the pyramid and the figure's feet), symbolizing that authenticity is the foundation for strength and vitality in all areas of life.

Luxury Cosmetics

The Brief

A vibrant digital reinterpretation of Botticelli's Birth of Venus, designed to communicate a message of Inherent Worth, Feminine Power, and Self-Actualization. The work asserts that every woman embodies the archetype of the Goddess (Deusa), emphasizing confidence and innate value.

The Concept

The Rebirth of the Inner Goddess. This concept updates the classical ideal of beauty and love with themes of modern female empowerment. By isolating the figure of Venus against a vivid pink frame and surrounding her with intense flora and fauna (the Hoopoe bird as a symbol of self-respect), the piece visually demands recognition. It transforms the mythological narrative into a declaration that every woman's emergence (in her career, life, or self-identity) is a moment of divine, powerful (Ri)Nascita (Rebirth).

Emotive Visuals

The Brief

A high-contrast digital collage centered on the Portuguese concept of Saudade (a deep longing or melancholy nostalgia). The piece focuses on the emotional experience of Cultural Absence, contrasting the individual's presence with a rich, stylized backdrop of Brazilian flora, fauna, and vivid colors.

The Concept

The Weight of Nostalgia. The composition places a subtly melancholic figure, ready for travel and/or recently returned, against an intensely saturated, almost dream-like frame of native elements (butterflies, tropical birds, fruits). The visual energy and warmth of the background emphasize the absence felt by the figure, creating a tension between the physical distance and the emotional attachment to the vibrant home culture. The overall message is an exploration of memory and the deep connection to one's origins.

Wellness & Calm

The Brief

A surrealist digital collage centered on the essential skill of Self-Awareness in career management. The piece specifically addresses the critical need for Proactive Rest and the setting of personal boundaries to prevent exhaustion.

The Concept

The Wisdom of Rest. The figure, depicted in grayscale to suggest the state of depletion or professional neutrality, is physically framed and shielded by transformative symbols (butterflies and coral/seaweed). This juxtaposition emphasizes that pausing is an active, necessary choice, not a failure. The poem highlights a deliberate, conscious decision-making process: the wisdom to discern when to step back ("give up") versus when to simply recharge ("rest"). The vibrant pink and textures contrast the figure, celebrating the act of self-care and renewal.
